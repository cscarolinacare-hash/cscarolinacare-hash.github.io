About Us

Hi! We're two responsible high school students based in Chapin SC, with a genuine love for animals. As dedicated students and pet owners ourselves, we understand how important it is to have someone you can trust caring for your pets. We offer reliable dog walking and pet sitting services with a focus on safety, attention and plenty of love. Our goal is to give pet parents peace of mind while keeping your pets happy and active!
